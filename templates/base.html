What is OpenVino?

The OpenVINO™ toolkit is an open source product. It contains the Deep Learning Deployment Toolkit (DLDT) for Intel® processors (for CPUs), Intel® Processor Graphics (for GPUs), and heterogeneous support. It includes an open model zoo with pretrained models, samples, and demos.

How does it work?

There are three models available on this webapp. Once you chose your model and Image, It is passed to Inference Engine of the OPENVINO Toolkit for your prediction.

How long does it take to run?

It takes approximately fifteen to thirty seconds to run.

How much does it cost?

It's free. It's an open source project.
